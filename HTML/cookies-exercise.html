We're going to borrow a nice picture from General mills

Here is what the recipe calls for

    Ingredients
  • 6 Eggs
  • 2 coups of flour
  • 2 Tablesppos of vanilla extract
  • 2 Teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 cup of sugar
    Instructions
  1. Add eggs, flour, vanila extract and mix
  2. Combine with sugar and baking powder and stir
  3. Eat half the cookie batter because its delicious
  4. Take the rest of the cookie batter and make cookies
  5. Bake at 350 for 24 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
